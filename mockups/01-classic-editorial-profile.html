Resident Profile — 12 Crestview Court Residents Since 1994

Margaret & Tom Ellis

Keepers of the village's finest rose beds, and of the first porch light of every summer.

Margaret taught third grade for thirty years and now runs the village book club with the same gentle authority. Tom, a retired civil engineer, can be found most mornings on the walking trail or fixing something that wasn't quite broken. They keep the finest rose beds in the village and host the first porch gathering of every summer.
