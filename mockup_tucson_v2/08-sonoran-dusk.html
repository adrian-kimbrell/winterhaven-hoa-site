CC&R Corner · Question of the Week

“Can I park my RV in my driveway overnight?”

Short answer: Yes — up to 72 hours for loading and unloading. Beyond that, RVs need to be stored off-site or in an enclosed garage.

Reference: CC&Rs, Section 4.3 — Vehicles & Parking
