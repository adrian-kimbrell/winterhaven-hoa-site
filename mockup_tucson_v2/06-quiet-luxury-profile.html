Margaret & Tom Ellis

12 Ocotillo Court · residents since 1994

Their Story

Margaret taught third grade for thirty years and now runs the village book club with the same gentle authority. Tom, a retired civil engineer, spends his mornings on the loop trail before the heat sets in, photographing whatever the desert offers up. Their courtyard garden — penstemon, agave, and one stubborn rosebush — wins the spring garden walk more years than not, and they host the first porch gathering of every monsoon season.
